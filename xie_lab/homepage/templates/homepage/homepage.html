About us

The Electron Microscope and Extreme Matter (EM) Lab is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of electron microscopy technology in order to tackle the unresolved issues in engineering systems that involve materials subjected to extreme conditions. Our lab is committed to developing and implementing cutting-edge techniques to help overcome the challenges faced in understanding and manipulating matter in such demanding environments.
